Marketing Lead

Type

Location

USA / Remote

Apply now

Come build the future of food and agriculture with Leaf as their Marketing Lead!

Our growing team is seeking someone who can lead the marketing function and shape the go-to-market strategy while leading the corresponding tactics.


What You’ll Do:

  • Lead the go-to-market team in building out customer segments and developing buyer personas for both C-suite/product leader and developer audiences

  • Own the development of all product positioning and messaging related to key customer segments and personas

  • Drive technical and business level messaging, both internally and externally, regarding our products and future product roadmap

  • Partner with product development and engineering teams through the product development cycle to help create our communication strategies to educate on new products and releases

  • Create and update a competitive analysis focused on the market competition’s pricing, value proposition, and go-to-market strategy

  • Collaborate with the sales teams to craft and review marketing and sales content and set the direction for dedicated marketing campaigns to promote product and/or features

  • Craft a customer journey map and distinguish key nurturing tactics and campaigns to move prospects further down the sales funnel

  • Work in conjunction with sales as related to pricing and corresponding offers/pricing-related campaigns. This includes development of sales enablement collateral such as sell sheets, slide decks, whitepapers, videos etc.

  • Facilitate and nurture a growing demand generation strategy while allocating budget to specific campaign use and incorporating demand gen experts contractors as necessary

  • Set, measure, analyze and report on marketing KPIs to show marketing attribution to deal pipeline, customer upsells, and overall brand impact

  • Manage three team members (content & community manager, graphic designer, front-end developer) as well as any freelance/contract support team members

Who You Are:

  • You have 5-7 years experience in various marketing roles in the enterprise software (SaaS) space. You have a solid understanding of brand management, product marketing, inbound marketing, and content marketing.

  • You have experience building out customer personas via market segments and mapping them to value propositions and message maps

  • You have solid knowledge of marketing analytics, automation, and content creation tools. You are up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in digital marketing.

  • You gained experience working with B2B software (ideally global) companies and you are familiar with the dynamics and customer journey in software companies.

  • You are technically fluent and are comfortable dealing with technical information, translating technical capabilities into customer benefits. You have proven ability to craft engaging content, including blog posts, white-papers, slide decks, videos, etc.

  • You are a tenacious self-starter with initiative and you have the ability to seamlessly communicate and collaborate with the product, marketing and sales teams.

Your background:

  • Bachelor’s Degree (B.S./B.A.) required, Master’s/MBA a plus. Relevant fields of study include Business Administration, Computer Science, Marketing
